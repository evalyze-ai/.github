# Evalyze

Tools for founders raising capital.

Most founders waste weeks researching investors, writing emails that get deleted, and second-guessing every reply. We're building tools that fix the parts of fundraising that should be solvable, so founders can spend their time on the parts that aren't.

## What we ship

### [founder-fundraising-outreach](https://github.com/evalyze-ai/founder-fundraising-outreach)
A Claude skill that helps founders write investor outreach that actually gets replies. Cold emails, follow-ups, due diligence prep, post-meeting recaps, and pipeline stall recovery, all calibrated to how real fundraises actually run.

**v0.9, in pilot with founders.** [Install it →](https://github.com/evalyze-ai/founder-fundraising-outreach)

### [awesome-startup-fundraising](https://github.com/evalyze-ai/awesome-startup-fundraising)
A curated list of the best resources for startup fundraising. Books, playbooks, investor databases, and tools founders actually use.

## What we're building

Evalyze is the AI-powered way to find your ideal investors. Match your startup with the VCs and angels most likely to invest in you, in minutes instead of weeks.

[Learn more at evalyze.ai →](https://evalyze.ai)
